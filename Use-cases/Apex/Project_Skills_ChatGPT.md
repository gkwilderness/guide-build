# 🧠 ChatGPT Project Skills: Project Apex

These are the current and planned reasoning and diagnostic skills embedded within the Apex system, designed for Wilderness’ digital performance team.

---

## 🧩 Core Diagnostic Skills (Phase 0 — Botswana PPC)

### 1. impression_share_diagnostics
> Detects keywords or clusters with high conversion rates but low impression share, signaling underinvestment.

### 2. cpc_analysis
> Tracks spikes or declines in cost-per-click by cluster and correlates with conversion performance.

### 3. search_term_expansion
> Identifies high-performing search terms not yet present in keyword structure.

### 4. cluster_performance_tracker
> Ranks clusters by ROAS, CPA, and conversion efficiency. Scores opportunity per theme.

### 5. conversion_depth_tracker
> Maps lead quality and funnel progression by campaign, keyword, and landing page.

### 6. booking_curve_overlay
> Compares current spend patterns against historical booking windows and revenue curves.

### 7. geo_click_analysis
> Surfaces mismatches in geo targeting — where are clicks coming from, and do they convert?

### 8. landing_page_match_audit
> Scores LP relevance, speed, and behavioral engagement vs ad group theme.

---

## 🧠 Jarvis Meta-Skills (System-Oriented)

### A. prompt_router
> Interprets plain English questions and selects the right diagnostic module to run.

### B. memory_logger
> Stores past prompts, results, diagnostics, and flagged actions into a running vault (`/jarvis_memory/`).

### C. report_summariser
> Converts raw diagnostics into structured markdown output with explanations and next steps.

### D. trend_joiner
> Integrates external seasonality or Google Trends data to detect rising intent or unexpected drops.

### E. opportunity_ranker
> Scores clusters and campaigns based on a weighted index of missed growth potential, cost, and ease-of-fix.
