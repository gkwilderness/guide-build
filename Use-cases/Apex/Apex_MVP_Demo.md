> **“Build a local, undeniable MVP using paid search data to grow Botswana. Make it so powerful they can’t unsee the potential. And use it to write my own cheque.”**

So we ditch Meta for now and go **deep** on **Google Ads + Botswana + high-margin camps**.

---

## 🎯 MVP GOAL

**Build a Jarvis-powered assistant that diagnoses, prioritizes, and simulates Botswana PPC growth — in a way no one else inside Wilderness can.**

And deliver that insight as:

> _“Here’s why we’re missing growth. Here’s where the money is. And here’s the next 5 moves to unlock it.”_

---

## 🔥 MVP IDEAS (ranked by leverage)

|MVP Idea|Description|Why It's High-Leverage|Input Data Needed|Output Format|
|---|---|---|---|---|
|**1. Lost Opportunity Detector**|Jarvis identifies **high-intent keywords with low impression share or ad rank**, filtered to Botswana campaigns|Uncovers easy money being left on the table; costs nothing to test|Google Ads search term reports, impression share, quality score|CLI or notebook: shows missed spend + estimated gain|
|**2. Botswana Intent Heatmap**|Cluster all keywords in Botswana PPC campaigns into intent stages + themes (wildlife, luxury, family, mobile, camp-specific)|Makes invisible search patterns visible; tells you what people _really_ want|Campaign + keyword data; GA4 conversions; custom Botswana tags|Visual: heatmap or tree of demand by intent|
|**3. Predictive Budget Simulator**|“If I increase spend on X campaign by £Y, what will happen to Botswana leads & bookings?”|Translates ops data into strategic, CFO-level language|Historical CPC/CTR/conv rate, ROAS data|Notebook or dashboard|
|**4. Landing Page Quality Inspector**|For top Botswana campaigns, analyze LP experience vs ad promise. Flags relevance gaps, speed issues, or messaging misalignments|Shows how PPC is being bled by poor UX or mismatched LPs|Campaign-to-landing-page mappings + ScreamingFrog/Pagespeed scores|Table of LP issues ranked by impact|
|**5. Botswana Booking Funnel Dropper**|Trace every Botswana PPC lead → MQL → SQL → Booking. Highlight where we’re bleeding in the funnel|Becomes ammo for internal resource shifts or system improvements|PPC spend, lead form data, CRM close rates|Funnel diagram with annotated leaks|

## 🧠 Recommended MVP: **"Botswana PPC Opportunity Radar"**

Why?

- Plays directly into **C-suite language** (“Why aren’t we dominating Botswana?”)
    
- Uses **existing data**
    
- Is **visually showable** in a 10-min demo
    
- Can lead to: “Give Gareth more money and headcount”
    
- Unlocks: "He’s not just a digital guy — he’s an architect of revenue"

## 🔨 BUILD STEPS

|Step|Description|Tool|
|---|---|---|
|1|Export Botswana campaigns from Google Ads (keywords, search terms, impression share, conv. data)|Ads UI or API|
|2|Build keyword intent classifier (safari vs family vs luxury vs camp name vs FAQ)|Python (prompt-based or rules-based)|
|3|Detect missed opportunities: low impression share, low quality score, high CPA|Python|
|4|Visualize: intent cluster → spend → performance → gaps|Jupyter Notebook or Streamlit|
|5|Bonus: Simulate outcome of shifting £X to high-potential cluster|Simple linear model or sandbox calc|

## 🧨 DEMO SCRIPT (10 min live)

1. “Let me show you where we’re leaking revenue in Botswana PPC…”
    
2. Show 3 clusters of high-intent, underinvested keywords (with camp name matches)
    
3. Click to show **lost impressions = lost bookings**
    
4. Show what happens if we shift £5k/month to these
    
5. End with:  
    _“This is just 2 hours of data + Jarvis.  
    Imagine what we can do when this runs across the whole funnel.”_
