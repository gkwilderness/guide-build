# 🧭 Project Brief: Apex

## 🎯 Objective
Build and deploy Apex — an AI tactical execution engine for PPC and paid media — starting with the Botswana Diagnostic Engine (Phase 0) and expanding to a full multi-brand performance intelligence system.

## 🧠 Strategic Context
Apex is the execution layer to CapitalCore's intelligence layer. Where CapitalCore tells you what's happening and why (yield curves, budget pacing risk, capital efficiency), Apex acts on it — automating diagnostics, surfacing anomalies, generating ad copy recommendations, and deploying tactical responses.

Apex is also a future external product: Apex-as-a-Service is Pillar 2 of the [[20-Projects/z__HOLD/Sovereign/00_Project-Brief]] escape velocity income stack.

The boundary between CapitalCore and Apex: CapitalCore = "what is the data saying?"; Apex = "what do we do about it?"

See [[CapitalCore vs Apex Boundaries]] for precise delineation.
