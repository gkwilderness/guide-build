# THE GAME WINNER: LEAD QUALITY ENGINEERING

## The Truth:
We don’t just need more leads.  
We need **better leads** — those who:
- Convert faster
- Spend more
- Require less persuasion
- Align with our luxury positioning

## Why It Matters:
- Better leads = higher ROI per click
- Sales team productivity multiplies
- PPC + Social channels train on better data
- Website & CRO efforts compound more efficiently

## The Strategy:
### Engineer for Buyer *Fit*, not Click Volume
Focus paid spend, creative, landing pages, and form flows around attracting and qualifying **only the most valuable buyers.**

---

## TACTICAL PRIORITY: 

| Lever         | Lead Quality Move                                           |
|---------------|-------------------------------------------------------------|
| PPC           | Rebuild around high-intent, high-spend, geo-qualified terms |
| Paid Social   | Use exclusive framing, not broad reach or destination ads   |
| Ad Copy       | Signal luxury and spend early; repel low-intent browsers    |
| Landing Pages | Add subtle disqualifiers + elevate tone of exclusivity      |
| Forms         | Qualify subtly (budget range, interests, urgency)           |
| CRO           | Prioritize UX that assumes value, not persuasion            |

---

## Target Outcome by September:
- +30–40% bookings
- Improved lead-to-booking CVR (6% → 8–9%)
- Stronger pipeline for Jan–March doubling

This isn’t just performance.  
**This is strategy in motion.**
