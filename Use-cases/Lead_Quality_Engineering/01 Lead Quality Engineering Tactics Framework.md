# Lead Quality Engineering Tactics Framework

## 1. Targeting & Intent Alignment
- Use exact match on “luxury safari,” “private safari,” “$20k+ safari”
- Exclude "budget", "cheap", "volunteer", "Kruger", etc.
- Apply geo-modifiers: HNW postcodes, wealth clusters

## 2. Messaging & Copy Precision
- Lead with legacy, conservation, privacy, exclusivity
- “Tailored journeys from $25,000+”
- “Private, off-grid, curated for discerning travellers”

## 3. Creative Strategy
- Hero shots = intimacy, isolation, elegance — not crowds or animals only
- Include aspirational signals: champagne in wild, legacy moments
- Avoid safari cliché imagery

## 4. Form & Funnel Filters
- Ask for: travel month, group size, interests, budget (in elegant language)
- Add concierge copy tone: “Our specialists will tailor an experience...”
- Pre-load sales team with lead intelligence

## 5. Website Tone & UX
- Full-screen luxury visuals, storytelling above features
- Use social proof: elite press coverage, awards, testimonials
- “Let’s plan something remarkable together” > “Enquire Now”
