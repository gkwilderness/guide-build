# Paid Media Structure — Lead Quality First

## PPC Campaign Tiers

### Tier 1: High-Intent, High-Value
- Exact match: “luxury safari Botswana”, “private African safari”
- Geo-restricted to top 5 wealth zones
- Bid aggressively, apply dayparting

### Tier 2: Retargeting Warm Leads
- Time-delayed ads with creative built for re-engagement
- Dynamic creative (by interest or itinerary)

### Tier 3: Disqualify or Pause
- Broad match with poor conversion
- Mid-intent terms not leading to bookings

## Paid Social Structure

### Awareness
- Run high-gloss creatives only with strong value framing
- Target by income, travel behaviours, lifestyle indicators

### Re-engagement
- Testimonials, story-based ads, remarketing with social proof

### Conversion
- Lead gen + Landing page ads with luxury hooks only

**Bidding Model:** Focus on **Value-Based Bidding** when possible — not CPC
**Attribution:** UTM rigor + CRM feedback loop for lead source quality
