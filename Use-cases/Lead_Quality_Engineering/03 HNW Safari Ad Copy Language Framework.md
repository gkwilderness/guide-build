# HNW Safari Ad Copy Language Framework

## Hook Phrases
- “Step into the wild, without leaving luxury behind.”
- “Private, custom safaris for discerning travellers.”
- “Crafted journeys. Remote places. Absolute privacy.”
- “Reconnect in Africa’s most exclusive wilderness.”

## Value Anchors
- “Tailored from $25,000” (sets bar early)
- “Limited access lodges — no crowds, no compromises”
- “Designed for families, founders, and legacy builders”
- “Unrushed. Unspoiled. Unforgettable.”

## Scarcity & Status
- “Only 4 departures left this season”
- “Experience camps reserved by royalty and rockstars”
- “When isolation becomes the ultimate indulgence”

## CTA Replacements
- “Let’s design your safari”  
- “Speak to your private guide planner”  
- “Start your legacy safari”  
- “Begin your wilderness journey”
