# CapitalCore — Beyond Firebreak Modules

## Purpose
High-leverage guardrails to extend CapitalCore beyond firebreak pacing/allocations. These modules protect capital and create defensibility across all three businesses.

---

### 1. Closed-Loop Signal Integration
- **What:** Import HubSpot lead stages, sales disqual reasons, call quality scores, and availability into Ads as conversion values.  
- **Why:** Train bidding on *booking probability* not raw CPL.  
- **Output:** "£1 leads worth £0.20 each removed from algo; high-quality leads fed back as £X."  

### 2. Cross-Brand Shared Intelligence
- **What:** Pool query → intent → outcome data across Wilderness, Jacada, YZ.  
- **Why:** Waste in one brand (e.g. "Kenya safari" at £700 CPL) becomes a negative across all.  
- **Output:** Shared negative/positive keyword lists auto-synchronised across brands.  

### 3. Temporal Intelligence (Velocity Curves)
- **What:** Map time-to-book per intent cluster (7 days vs 70 days).  
- **Why:** Allocate budget to faster-converting demand when liquidity is tight.  
- **Output:** "Cluster X books in 8.2 days, Cluster Y in 61.4 days → shift £Z."  

### 4. Auction Adversary Tracking
- **What:** Parse Auction Insights daily.  
- **Why:** Detect competitor overbidding/weakness → adjust budgets opportunistically.  
- **Output:** "Competitor ABC dropped IS 20% yesterday in UK → recommend +15% budget."  

### 5. Intent × Wealth / Access Overlays
- **What:** Enrich queries with Wealth Index (Windfall/Experian) + flight access data.  
- **Why:** Only chase demand that’s actually serviceable by wealth + connectivity.  
- **Output:** "Excluding 40% of queries from 2-stop markets lifted booking rate +18%."  

### 6. Explainability Layer (Investor Narrative)
- **What:** Auto-generate board-safe narratives from diagnostics.  
- **Why:** Make capital protection legible to ExCo/PE.  
- **Output:** "This week, £47k spend avoided via exclusion of Kenya/South Africa terms at £600+ CPL."
