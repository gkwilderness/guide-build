## **1. Elevate Conversion Quality & Feedback Loop (Offline Conversions or Lead Scoring)**

**Why it’s high leverage:**  
Smart Bidding only works as well as the signals it’s fed. Most accounts scale **inefficiently** because they optimize for **any form fill**, not _qualified_ leads or bookings.

**What to do:**

- Connect your **CRM or booking team feedback** to Google Ads using **offline conversion import (OCI)**.
    
- If that’s not yet possible, tag leads by **scoring tiers** (e.g., “Sales accepted”, “Tour requested”, “Unqualified”) and import as secondary conversions.
    
- Prioritize TOFU/MOFU bid signals based on which terms/audiences **actually convert to revenue**.
    

**Why this works:**  
It stops wasted budget scaling low-value leads and supercharges the algorithm with real revenue signals. That’s how you unlock 2–4x ROAS jumps as you scale.

---

## **2. Intent-Tiered Campaign Restructure + Budget Reallocation**

**Why it’s high leverage:**  
Campaigns that blend TOFU, MOFU, and BOFU keywords confuse both users _and_ the algorithm. Traffic is expensive, so **every dollar must align with intent**.

**What to do:**

- Restructure into **intent-based campaigns**: e.g. `BOFU_Exact`, `MOFU_Phrase`, `TOFU_Broad`.
    
- Pair each with the right **bidding strategy** (e.g., tROAS for BOFU, Max Conversions for MOFU, Max Clicks w/ audience for TOFU).
    
- Use shared negatives to **prevent overlap/cannibalization**.
    
- Allocate **70–80% of budget to BOFU + MOFU**, and run **tightly audience-gated TOFU tests**.
    

**Why this works:**  
You stop spending blind across the funnel and focus on segments that drive qualified demand — a critical multiplier when scaling in a high-CPL market.

---

## **3. Aggressive Creative Testing on RSA + LP Combinations**

**Why it’s high leverage:**  
Even if your targeting is perfect, **ad copy and landing pages** determine:

- Who clicks (qualified vs. curious)
    
- How well they convert
    
- How the auction treats your ads (via Quality Score & CTR)
    

**What to do:**

- Test 5–10 **high-intent RSAs** per funnel stage, focused on _benefits_, _urgency_, _social proof_, _impact_.
    
- Align ad variants to **geo, persona, or product themes** (e.g. “Botswana”, “Private Safari”, “Gorilla Trekking”).
    
- Build out 3–5 **custom LP variants**:
    
    - BOFU: Price & availability-focused
        
    - MOFU: Value prop & USPs
        
    - TOFU: Emotional storytelling + itinerary inspiration
        

**Why this works:**  
High-quality RSAs + LPs = more clicks from the right people + higher CVR = lower CAC. Especially in luxury, narrative and exclusivity matter more than specs.

---

## Bonus: What _Not_ to Focus On Right Now

- Obsessing over bid caps before conversion quality is optimized.
    
- Sprawling Broad Match campaigns without audience control.
    
- Running TOFU display/YouTube campaigns if bottom-funnel isn’t converting profitably yet.
