## 🔧 Budget Allocation Strategy Overview

### **1. Funnel-Based Split (Starting Point)**

|Funnel Stage|Campaigns|% Allocation|Monthly Budget|
|---|---|---|---|
|**BOFU**|Branded & Camp-Specific|45%|$90,000|
|**MOFU**|Geo + category-specific|40%|$80,000|
|**TOFU**|Broad + audience-gated discovery|15%|$30,000|
|**TOTAL**||**100%**|**$200,000**|

---

### **2. BOFU ($90k/month) – Booking Intent, High Efficiency**

- Focus: Exact match, high-converting terms, branded & camp-specific.
    
- Campaigns:
    
    - `Brand_Wilderness_Exact`
        
    - `Camp_Mombo_Exact`
        
    - `Camp_LittleVumbura_Exact`
        

**Tactics**:

- Use **Target ROAS or CPA** bidding.
    
- Set higher budgets on top-performers (based on past ROAS).
    
- Ensure offline conversion signals are flowing.
    

---

### **3. MOFU ($80k/month) – Qualified Category/Geo Interest**

- Focus: Phrase match, intent-qualified category/location queries.
    
- Campaigns:
    
    - `Luxury_Safari_Botswana_Phrase`
        
    - `Private_Safari_Namibia_Phrase`
        
    - `Gorilla_Trekking_Rwanda_Phrase`
        

**Tactics**:

- Use **Max Conversions or Target CPA**.
    
- Layer **in-market** and **GA4 audience segments**.
    
- Route to **high-value LPs with USPs**, not just generic content.
    
- Evaluate spend based on **lead quality**, not just volume.
    

---

### **4. TOFU ($30k/month) – Discovery, Top of Funnel**

- Focus: Broad match, inspiration keywords + custom audiences.
    
- Campaigns:
    
    - `Explore_Luxury_Safaris_Broad`
        
    - `African_Safari_Ideas_Broad`
        

**Tactics**:

- Use **Max Clicks with CPC cap** or test **Max Conversions with audience gating**.
    
- Limit to **top GA4 custom segments**, **affinity audiences**, and/or **high-value lookalikes**.
    
- Evaluate based on **assisted conversion**, **scroll depth**, or **micro-goals** (not just last-click).
    

---

### **5. Intra-Funnel Adjustments (Monthly/Quarterly)**

|Funnel|When to Reallocate More Budget|
|---|---|
|BOFU|When CVR is strong, CPL is stable, or CRM confirms high lead value.|
|MOFU|When assisted conversions grow or new geo/themes emerge as ROAS-positive.|
|TOFU|Only scale once 1st/2nd click attribution proves high contribution to booked revenue.|

---

## ⚙ Optimization Feedback Loop

Use this monthly routine:

1. **Review performance by funnel tier**:
    
    - CPA / ROAS / Lead Quality (offline)
        
2. **Refine allocation weekly/monthly**:
    
    - +10–20% to top-performing MOFU/BOFU campaigns
        
    - -10–20% from non-converting TOFU experiments
        
3. **Feed data into bidding strategies**:
    
    - Offline conversions → Google Ads
        
    - Engagement tiers → GA4/CM → audience layering
