# 🧭 Project Brief: Paid Media

## 🎯 Objective
Build a best-in-class, AI-augmented paid media operation for Wilderness Group — optimised for lead quality over lead volume, targeting UHNWI and HNWI travellers across Google Ads, Meta, and programmatic channels.

## 🧠 Strategic Context
Paid media is the primary acquisition engine for qualified luxury travel leads. The strategic shift is from CPL (cost per lead) optimisation to LQS (lead quality score) optimisation — a structural change enabled by CapitalCore's intelligence layer and the UHNWI ICP.

Apex is the tactical AI deployment engine that drives paid media execution: automated diagnostics, performance pattern detection, budget pacing, and copy generation.

The team: Danny (Group Head of Performance), Fay (Senior Performance Marketer — trajectory: Head of Performance), Claire (Senior PPC Manager), Frances (Performance Marketing Manager).

## 🏗️ Channel Architecture

## 🏗️ AI Ad Copy System (Reference Model)
From [[AI_Ad_Copy]] — Obvi CMO framework:
1. Fuel with raw customer language (reviews, surveys, CS tickets)
2. Extract personas via AI (psychologist + direct response marketer prompt)
3. Map product benefits to personas
4. Analyse top-performing ads
5. Generate copy components per persona (headlines, subheads, body, CTAs)
6. Human polish and creative direction
